about me

hello, world!

My name is Maketh Poul, and my journey has been shaped by resilience, determination, and a desire to create a better future. I was born during the civil war in Sudan, a time of conflict and uncertainty that deeply affected many families, including my own. Seeking safety and hope, my parents relocated to Kakuma Refugee Camp, where I spent much of my childhood and completed my education.

Growing up in a refugee camp came with many challenges. Opportunities were limited, and after finishing school, pursuing higher education was not immediately possible. Despite these obstacles, I remained focused on building a better future for myself. Rather than allowing circumstances to define my path, I chose to invest in learning and personal development.

Since last year, I have been acquiring practical technology skills through action of Refugee life, where I continue to develop my knowledge in software development and digital technologies. This experience has strengthened my passion for technology and motivated me to pursue a career as a software engineer. I am committed to continuous learning, problem-solving, and creating solutions that can make a positive impact on people's lives.

Beyond technology, I am passionate about football. The sport has taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, discipline, perseverance, and leadership. Football has always been more than a game to me—it is a source of motivation, unity, and personal growth. The same dedication and determination that I bring to the football field are the qualities I apply to my learning journey and professional aspirations.

My story is one of overcoming adversity, embracing opportunities, and striving for excellence. I am proud of my background and the experiences that have shaped me into the person I am today. With a strong foundation in resilience and a passion for technology, I am excited about the future and the possibilities that lie ahead as I continue to grow and contribute to the world of software engineering.

Today, I am focused on growing my skills, building meaningful projects, and creating opportunities for myself and others. I believe that challenges can become stepping stones to success, and I am determined to use my experiences, skills, and passion to make a positive contribution to the world through technology.
